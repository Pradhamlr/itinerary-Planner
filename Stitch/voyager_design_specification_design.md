# Design System Specification: Voyager (Horizon Ethos)

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star: "The Digital Concierge"
Voyager is an AI-powered smart itinerary planner that balances data-rich ML insights with a clean, luxury travel aesthetic. The goal is to make complex scheduling feel effortless and inspiring.

## 2. Visual Language
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy (#001F3F) for authority, Teal (#0D9488) for action/highlights, and soft Slate grays for UI structure.
- **Typography:** *Plus Jakarta Sans* for a modern, tech-forward yet approachable feel. High contrast between headers and body text.
- **Shape & Form:** Soft, rounded corners (8px/12px) and subtle depth through layered backgrounds rather than heavy shadows.
- **Imagery:** High-quality, immersive travel photography to evoke emotion and desire.

## 3. Core User Flows
- **Discovery:** Browse destinations and saved trips from a high-level dashboard.
- **Planning:** A guided, step-by-step onboarding for new trips (Vibe Selection, Budget, Dates).
- **Curation:** Reviewing AI-generated recommendations (ML-scored) before finalizing.
- **Management:** Interactive day-by-day itineraries with integrated map routing.

## 4. Key Components
- **TopNavBar:** Fixed navigation with search and profile management.
- **SideNavBar:** Contextual navigation for specific trips (Daily Plan, Map, Budget).
- **Cards:** Specialized cards for Destinations (with immersive images) and Activities (with ML scores and time slots).
- **Modals/Overlays:** Used for quick edits and detailed activity views without losing context.
